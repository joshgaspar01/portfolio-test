Naturehop

NatureHop leads enganging nature walks for NatureHop members around the natural areas of the Greater Toronto Area every weekend. Led by a group of naturalists and volunteers with varied and expansive knowlege of the local ecosystems. On these walks you will learn all about the adaptation of wildlife to the urban environment and what you can do to preserve it.

Frequently asked questions

Q: Can I take pictures on the nature walks?
A: You can bring your own camera or borrow a Naturehop camera to snap pictures of any wildlife observed on the walk

Q: What will I learn on the walks?
A: Our walk leaders will teach you how to treat the wildlife and plant life that populates the Greater Toronto Area

Q: Is it okay to feed the wild animals?
A: The leaders of the nature walks can teach you about which foods are safe to feed to which animals. If you are not sure if you should feed an animal, ask!
