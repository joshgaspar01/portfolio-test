What is conServ?

conServ is a worldwide initiative to save species on the brink of extinction. In the current political landscape, politicians accross Canada and the United States are becoming more and more regressive with laws that prevent the hunting of endangered species. With your help we are rebuilding self-sustaining ecosystems around the world from the ground up and fighting for these laws. With your help, we can keep these species in existence.
