Statement

conServ believes in the power of humans to do good, and would like to thank everyone who exercises their ability to do good through donating to our cause.

At conServ we maintain a level of transparency that allows you to donate without worry, as your money is going directly towards the conservation of endangered species.

Over the past year you have helped us reach new levels of conservation with the start of our new giant panda rehabilitation program that has rehabilitated over 50 pandas into the wild already and the continuation of our other conservation programs that have helped a range of species over the years including sea otters, gorillas and asian elephants.

Ever since our first program to save the Asian elephants started in 2010, we’ve made great progress towards more sustainable wildlife.

President & CEO of conServ
Madeline Atkins
